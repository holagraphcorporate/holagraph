REACH Student Partner Programme

creating leaders of tomorrow

Who are we ?


REACH Student Partner Programme (RSPP) is envisioned as a multi-tiered network that offers a multi-year, graded, progression path that will enable your personal and professional growth through a set of activities, access to peer-to-peer network, mentorship and access to a range of economic,learning and social opportunities.

While many activities of RSPP are built around creating awareness about the benefits of REACH products, the majority of these activities do not have any direct or immediate benefit to REACH. For eg., through RSPP you will get to connect with other students and mentors who could help you acquire a new skill or improve a skill you already have, discover new opportunities or gain visibility in your chosen domain.

What will you do as a REACH Student Partner?

  • Represent and promote REACH on your own campus.
  • Identify and choose a field of work as a student partner (graphic design, UI/UX, marketing, content writing, HR, entrepreneurship, and much more). 
  • Complete tasks in the allotted field on a regular basis.
  • Identify competent students to join the student partnership program and build your network.
  • Avail and make full use of the training programs conducted by REACH. 
  • Ideate promotional strategies and effective collaborations on campus or for an event. 
  • Have good knowledge about the products REACH offers.

What will you get as a REACH Student Partner?

  • Option to work in different areas of expertise as a student partner  (graphic design, UI/UX, marketing, content writing, HR, entrepreneurship, and much more). 
  • Ability to grow as a student partner into various leadership roles (Manager, Vice President, President) within REACH Student Partnership Programme. 
  • Wholesome mentorship and development of student partners (leadership, communication, entrepreneurship, domain, etc) by leaders of various domains.
  • Official REACH merchandise. 
  • Opportunity to participate and win in incentive programs launched by REACH.
  • Certificate and Letter of Recommendation.
  • Access to learn and connect with senior industry professionals and craft your own career with under their guidance.  

Eligibility

  • You must be a current student of any institution.
  • You must be able to conduct seminars 
  • You must be active on social media and on-campus

Duration: 3 months/6 months/12 months
