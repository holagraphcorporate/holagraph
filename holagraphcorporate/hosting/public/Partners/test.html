As a Domain Expert, you can help many aspiring professionals learn additional skills to perform their current and future job roles more effectively. Indeed, for many of them, this could lead to a transformational orbit change in their careers.
Such skill training can happen at timings that are convenient to you. These could be a couple of hours a week or a full-time job. Some training can be delivered completely online and on phone whereas some other training may need to combine online + offline delivery. In cases, where the training needs offline delivery as well, we will help you connect with infrastructure partners of holagraph whose classrooms, conference rooms and IT and other training infrastructure you would be able to use.
