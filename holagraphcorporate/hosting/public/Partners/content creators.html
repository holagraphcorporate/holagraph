Skill Gaps

More than 15 million Indian youth enter the labour force every year. But
many of them have Skill Gaps and are not job-ready or are unemployable.


Leaving these Skill Gaps unfilled can lead to many challenges

Difficulty in securing a job or a freelance project.

Lower earnings.

Lower quality products causing loss of business and loss of reputation.

Delay in bringing new productlines to market.

Increase operating costs and make it difficult to introduce new practices.

Talent turnover and increased recruitment costs.

Current challenges in filling these
Skill Gaps

Often, students and professionals continue their career journeys without plugging these Skill Gaps due to multiple reasons

Once working, difficult to take time out to attend a training course.

May not have a training centre nearby.

May not have a training centre that offers training at timings convenient to her/him.

Courses available are full-fledged courses whereas she/he needs to learn just the skills she/he has gaps in.

Courses available may not be available in a language of instruction he/she would like it to be in.

They may not have the required equipment (e.g., laptop + licensed software) or may not have the funds or credit rating to secure a loan to buy the equipment.

This is where you
step in

As a Content Creator, you can help many aspiring professionals learn additional skills to perform their current and future job roles more effectively. You can also help small business owners expand their business. Indeed, for many of them, this could lead to a transformational orbit change in their careers. You can work at your convenience. This could be a couple of hours a week or a full-time job.

You can support freelancers, part-time/full-time professionals and small business owners in a number of ways

Develop engaging content for the course curriculum for training institutes

Ideate, shoot and edit educational videos for training institutes for better content retention

Create engaging digital content to help small business owners establish a strong brand online and increase engagement

Promote services and products of small and medium businesses by developing copy for websites, social media, and other channels

Brainstorm and create short-term and long-term content and marketing strategy by collaborating and coordinating with the business owners.

Offer career coaching (career trajectory, skills needed, etc.)services for individuals in the field of content creation

Offer career coaching (career trajectory, skills needed, etc.) services for a group of professionals or freelancers in the field of content creation
