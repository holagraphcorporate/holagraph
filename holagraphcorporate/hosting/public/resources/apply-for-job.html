How to apply for jobs on holagraph?

1


To search for jobs you can click on the search bar on the top or click on the third icon from the right side of the home icon.


2


If you click on the icon, you will see three options called Search Jobs, Saved Jobs and Applied Jobs.


3


When you click on Search Jobs option, you can type in the type of job you are looking for, e.g.: developer, designer, coder, etc.


4


Then you will get the job options which are available. You can apply for any of the jobs which you think matches your expectations.


5


Read all the details properly and then click on apply. You will have to add in some details, like a message for the employer and your resume. Once all this is done, click on apply again.


6


Lastly, when you click on Applied Jobs, you will see all the jobs where you have applied and you can also see their status.


Perfect! You have successfully applied for the job.
