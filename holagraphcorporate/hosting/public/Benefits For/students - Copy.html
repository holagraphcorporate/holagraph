If you are a student, holagraph will provide you the tools and support to kick start your career.

holagraph benefits for Students

  • Search and apply for jobs that match your skills
  • Make more informed career decisions with the help of data available on jobs in India segregated sector-wise, job role wise and district wise
  • Search and apply to skill training courses to upskill and get industry-ready. Skills training information available: sector-wise, job-wise, and district wise. Courses with job certainty are also available.
  • Search and apply for education loans
  • Collaborate with other students and freelancers and jointly execute projects for buyers.
  • Network with industry professionals, domain experts, mentors, and career coaches in your location and beyond to understand and learn the skills required to succeed in the industry.
  • Find remote Internships from several countries in multiple roles to increase industry exposure and earn.
  • Find part-time job opportunities near you.
  • Search and apply for equipment loans
  • Use holagraph SaaS workflow tools to keep track of all your projects.
  • Benefit from career coaching by our HR Consultant Partners
  • Enhance your chances of selection for jobs/projects/internships through cv building and interview workshops conducted by our Domain Expert Partners
